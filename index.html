What is Climate Change ?


Climate change is the long-term alteration of temperature and typical weather patterns in a place. Climate change could refer to a particular location or the planet as a whole. Climate change may cause weather patterns to be less predictable. These unexpected weather patterns can make it difficult to maintain and grow crops in regions that rely on farming because expected temperature and rainfall levels can no longer be relied on.It has also been connected with other damaging weather events such as more frequent and more intense hurricanes, floods, downpours, and winter storms.

Causes

There are various causes of climate change both natural and manmade.

Effects

Climate change has a wide range of effects on the environment, ecosystems, and human societies. These effects manifest in various ways and impact different regions differently.

Solutions

There are no standard solutions to stop climate change, but each one of us can contribute to tackle the issue.
